Your journey with a little legend begins here.

Adopting a little legend doesn’t cost a thing — our focus is finding loving homes. After you complete your application, we’ll arrange a magical meeting so you can see which creature is the perfect fit. We’ve also included a beginners friendly rating with our creatures so that you can gauge if you are ready to make them a part of your family.
